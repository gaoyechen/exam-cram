# Exam Type Patterns

Common exam types and their specific speed-cram strategies. Match the user's exam to the closest pattern.

---

## Pattern 1: Standardized Multiple Choice (标准化选择题)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 四六级 CET-4/6、公务员行测、驾考科一科四、计算机等级考试、证券从业
- 🌍 GRE/GMAT、IT certifications (AWS/Azure/PMP)、driving theory

**Characteristics:**
- Fixed options (typically 4 choices)
- Single correct answer
- Often has pattern in question distribution
- Scoring: usually no penalty for wrong answers (check first!)

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Don't study everything** — identify the top 5-6 topic areas that account for 70%+ of questions
2. **Pattern recognition** — do 3-5 past papers, identify recurring question types
3. **Technique-heavy** — elimination, keyword recognition, and probability guessing are your primary weapons
4. **Time is the real enemy** — practice under timed conditions more than studying content

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Content study: 30%
- Technique training: 40%
- Timed practice: 30%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, technique can compensate for up to 30-40% of missing knowledge. A student with good exam technique and mediocre knowledge often outperforms a student with good knowledge and poor technique.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 公考行测：言语理解和判断推理占比最大（~55%），优先攻这两块
- 四六级：听力占35%，用"预读选项法"提前看选项预判内容
- 证券/基金从业：法规题占60%+，直接刷题比看教材效率高3倍

## Pattern 2: Short Answer / Fill-in-the-Blank (简答/填空题)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 考研政治主观题、教师资格综合素质、医学考试主观题、自考简答题
- 🌍 High school finals, university midterms, professional qualification written sections

**Characteristics:**
- Requires recall, not just recognition
- Partial credit often available
- Keywords matter more than complete sentences
- Scoring: rubric-based, often keyword-driven

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Keyword harvesting** — for each topic, identify 5-8 key terms that graders look for
2. **Template answers** — pre-build answer templates for common question patterns
3. **Breadth over depth** — it's better to know a little about everything than a lot about a few topics
4. **Partial credit maximization** — always write something, even if incomplete

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Content study: 50%
- Technique training: 20%
- Timed practice: 30%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, knowing the right keywords is often sufficient. A well-structured answer with 3-4 correct keywords will score 60-80% even without deep understanding.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 考研政治：答题模板"是什么→为什么→怎么做"三段式，踩关键词就能拿70%分
- 教资综合素质：三观（教育观/学生观/教师观）是必考大题，背模板+结合材料即可
- 医学考试：诊断公式（症状+体征→诊断）比死记硬背有效

## Pattern 3: Essay / Long-Form (论述/作文题)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 考研政治论述题、申论、高考语文作文、MBA论说文
- 🌍 Graduate entrance essays, MBA essays, language proficiency writing sections

**Characteristics:**
- Extended writing required
- Structure and argumentation matter
- Grading is somewhat subjective
- Scoring: rubric-based but influenced by presentation

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Template mastery** — learn 3-4 essay templates (compare/contrast, problem-solution, argumentative, analytical)
2. **Outline first** — spend 5 minutes planning before writing
3. **Keyword density** — include subject-specific terminology throughout
4. **Structure over content** — a well-structured mediocre essay scores higher than a poorly-structured brilliant one

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Content study: 40%
- Template practice: 30%
- Timed writing: 30%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, structure is king. A 5-paragraph essay with clear intro-body-conclusion and topic sentences will score well even if the content is shallow.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 申论：政府工作报告式表达（"加强…推进…完善…"），格式比内容重要
- 考研政治：材料分析=原理+方法论+结合材料，三步缺一不可
- 高考作文：首段定调+三个分论点+尾段升华，名言素材各准备5个万能的

## Pattern 4: Problem-Solving / Calculation (计算/解题题)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 考研数学、高考数学/物理/化学、CPA财管/会计计算题、CFA计算题
- 🌍 Math/physics/engineering exams, financial calculations

**Characteristics:**
- Requires applying formulas/methods
- Step-by-step scoring common
- Show-your-work format
- Scoring: points for each correct step

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Formula cheat sheet** — compile all formulas on 1-2 pages
2. **Problem type recognition** — identify the 5-6 most common problem types
3. **Template solutions** — for each problem type, memorize the solution steps
4. **Show all work** — even if you get the wrong answer, correct steps earn partial credit

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Content study: 40%
- Formula memorization: 20%
- Problem practice: 40%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, partial credit is your best friend. Even if you can't solve a problem completely, showing the correct approach can earn 50-70% of the points.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 考研数学：高数占56%，线代22%，概率22%。高数是大头，优先攻极限、导数、积分
- CPA会计：分录是核心，掌握"借贷必相等"模板+常见业务分录，计算题拿60%
- CFA：计算器操作熟练度=时间效率，必须练到不用看计算器

## Pattern 5: Practical / Hands-On (实操/机考题)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 软考（程序员/网工等）、计算机二级、CPA机考、法律职业资格机考
- 🌍 Programming exams, lab exams, IT practical certifications

**Characteristics:**
- Requires actual execution, not just knowledge
- Often has a working environment (computer, lab, vehicle)
- Partial credit for partial completion
- Scoring: output-based or step-based

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Environment familiarization** — know the interface/environment before exam day
2. **Standard operations drill** — practice the 10 most common operations until automatic
3. **Error recovery** — learn how to recover from common mistakes (undo, reset, restart)
4. **Time management** — allocate time per task, never exceed it

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Environment familiarization: 20%
- Standard operations practice: 50%
- Full simulation: 30%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, muscle memory > theoretical knowledge. Practice the actual operations until they're automatic.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- CPA机考：必须提前熟悉考试系统界面，打字速度直接影响主观题完成度
- 软考：下午案例题是关键，上午选择题可靠排除法，下午必须会写
- 计算机二级：Excel函数和VBA是拉开差距的关键，选择题靠刷题

## Pattern 6: Oral / Interview (口试/面试)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 教资面试/试讲、考研复试、公务员面试、雅思口语
- 🌍 Language proficiency oral exams, thesis defense, professional certification interviews

**Characteristics:**
- Real-time interaction with examiner
- Confidence and fluency matter
- Structured responses expected
- Scoring: holistic impression + content accuracy

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Common questions prep** — prepare answers for the 20 most likely questions
2. **Structured responses** — use "First... Second... Finally..." format
3. **Filler phrases** — memorize transition phrases to buy thinking time
4. **Confidence practice** — rehearse in front of mirror or record yourself

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Content preparation: 30%
- Response structuring: 30%
- Rehearsal: 40%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, delivery is as important as content. A confident, well-structured response with average content scores higher than a hesitant, disorganized response with excellent content.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 教资面试：试讲=导入(1min)+新授(3min)+巩固(1min)+小结(1min)，缺环节直接扣分
- 公考面试：结构化面试=综合分析+计划组织+应急应变+人际关系，每类准备1个万能模板
- 考研复试：自我介绍+专业基础+研究计划，自我介绍练到脱口而出

## Pattern 7: Open Book / Take-Home (开卷/带回家考)

**Examples:**
- 🇨🇳 部分大学期末开卷考、在职研究生开卷考、部分法律考试
- 🌍 Some university exams, professional development assessments

**Characteristics:**
- Access to materials allowed
- Questions are harder / more analytical
- Time is usually more generous
- Scoring: emphasis on analysis and application

**Speed-cram strategy:**
1. **Organize materials** — create an index/cheat sheet for quick reference
2. **Understand the framework** — know WHERE to find answers, not WHAT the answers are
3. **Practice analytical questions** — the exam tests application, not recall
4. **Time management** — don't spend too long looking up answers

**Optimal time allocation:**
- Material organization: 30%
- Framework understanding: 30%
- Practice questions: 40%

**Critical rule:** For this exam type, organization beats knowledge. A student with well-organized materials and good search skills will outperform a student with better knowledge but poor organization.

**中国考试特别提示：**
- 开卷考的关键是"目录索引+标签贴"：提前在教材上贴好标签，考场能5秒翻到
- 很多开卷考的真实难点是时间不够翻书，所以核心知识点依然要记住

## Quick Pattern Matching Guide

Ask the user:
1. "Are the questions mostly multiple choice?" → Pattern 1
2. "Do you need to write short answers or fill in blanks?" → Pattern 2
3. "Do you need to write long essays?" → Pattern 3
4. "Do you need to solve math/physics/engineering problems?" → Pattern 4
5. "Do you need to perform tasks on a computer or in a lab?" → Pattern 5
6. "Do you need to speak or present?" → Pattern 6
7. "Can you use your books/materials during the exam?" → Pattern 7

**If you can identify the exam by name, skip the questions and match directly:**

| 四六级 CET | 1 (选择) | 3 (作文) |
| 公考行测 | 1 (选择) | — |
| 公考申论 | 3 (论述) | — |
| 考研政治 | 1 (选择) + 2 (简答) | 3 (论述) |
| 考研数学 | 4 (计算) | — |
| 考研英语 | 1 (选择) | 3 (作文) |
| CPA | 1 (选择) + 4 (计算) + 5 (机考) | — |
| 法考 | 1 (选择) + 5 (机考) | 2 (简答) |
| 教资笔试 | 1 (选择) + 2 (简答) | — |

If the exam is a mix of types, identify the dominant type (>50% of questions) and apply that pattern's strategy primarily, with elements from secondary patterns as needed.
